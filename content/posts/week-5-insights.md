## Buccaneers @ Bears

Excellent use of crossing routes from these Buccaneers.

Chicago's failure to pass the ball is almost entirely on Nick Foles here, not because of Tampa Bay's pass coverage. Tampa Bay's coverage unit seems like a liability that can be readily exploited by an able quarterback.
